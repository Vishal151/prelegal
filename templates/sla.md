# Service Level Agreement

1. ## Uptime
    1. ### Target Uptime
    Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Cloud Service available at or above the Target Uptime level, calculated on a monthly basis.

    2. ### Calculating Uptime
    Uptime is calculated as: (Available Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Available Minutes, measured monthly, with partial months prorated accordingly.

2. ## Response Time
    1. ### Target Response Time
    Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to respond to support requests within the Target Response Time through the designated Support Channel.

    2. ### Calculating Response Time
    Response time is measured from when Customer submits a support request to the Support Channel until Provider or Provider's support representative specifically acknowledges the request. Automated responses do not count.

3. ## Remedies
    1. ### Service Credit
    Customer is eligible for a Service Credit when uptime falls below the Target Uptime or support requests are not acknowledged within the Target Response Time. Service Credits apply only to future Cloud Service Fees.

    2. ### Requesting A Service Credit
    Customer must notify Provider within 7 days of month-end to request a Service Credit. Uptime Credit requests must include details about access failures; Response Time Credit requests must include documentation of contact attempts.

    3. ### Service Credit Limitations
    Credits cannot be converted to cash, do not accumulate beyond 8% of subscription fees per period, and expire at Order Form termination.

    4. ### Termination
    If Provider fails to meet the Target Uptime for 2 of 3 consecutive months (with proper notification), Customer may terminate the affected Order Form and receive a prorated refund of prepaid Fees.

    5. ### Exclusive Remedy
    This SLA represents Customer's exclusive remedy and Provider's entire liability for performance failures.

4. ## Definitions
    - **Available Minutes**: Monthly minutes minus Excluded Minutes and Scheduled Downtime.
    - **Downtime Minutes**: Minutes when the Cloud Service is unavailable per internal monitoring, minus exclusions.
    - **Excluded Minutes**: Outages resulting from Force Majeure Events, internet issues, third-party equipment failures, or unauthorized use.
    - **Service Credit**: The combined Uptime Credit plus Response Time Credit.
    - **SLA**: These Service Level Agreement Standard Terms as incorporated into the Order Form.
    - **SLA Standard Terms**: Common Paper Service Level Agreement Version 2.0.
